Cake Raffle App

The Cake Raffle app is a fun and interactive way for users to participate in raffles and potentially win delicious cakes. Users can easily enter their names into the raffle, and the app randomly selects a winner from the pool of participants. The app ensures fairness and transparency in the selection process.

BarkerBox

"BarkerBox" is an innovative subscription service designed to cater to the needs and preferences of beloved furry companions. This service offers a curated selection of high-quality toys, treats, and accessories tailored to dogs of all breeds, sizes, and ages.
